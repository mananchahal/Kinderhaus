About Kinderhaus

A Non Profit Initiative by a 15 yr old who saw many children disconnected from school for the last 6 months and with no clear safe ecosystem in sight.

The world has changed and forever. We will never be able to get back to the old methods. The risks are simply too great to be ignored. While the Institutions evolve and align their approach to the drastically changed scenario, each family has faced exponential levels of uncertainty and quite frankly there are no solutions in sight. The young children up to class 5th might not really understand the threat we face and it cannot be expected that they follow sanitization and prevention practices properly. Life for our children should be full of joy and fun -all they want is to be happy and play.
